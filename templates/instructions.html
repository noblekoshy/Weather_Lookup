    Instructions
  • Use the navbar on the top of the page to navigate the site.
  • This app looks up the weather by City Name. There are 3 options for entering in data: Enter City name in text bar, get random city name, or use current location
  • For entering in City name in text bar: Type name into bar and press Submit.
  • For Random City: Press the Random City button.
  • For Current Location: Press the Current Location button. Then refresh the page.
  • The Weather Result will be displayed on the Saved Cities section of the home page
  • To delete a saved city, press Delete
